FAQ

Common Questions

What if I need changes mid-project?

Minor adjustments within scope are no problem. For significant scope changes, I'll provide a quick quote for the additional work before proceeding — so there are never surprises on your bill.

Do you offer hosting and domain services?

I don't sell hosting directly, but I'll guide you through choosing the best provider for your needs (e.g., Safaricom Cloud, Truehost Kenya, or international providers) and handle the full setup for you at no extra charge.

What about ongoing maintenance?

Every project includes free post-launch support (7–60 days depending on tier). After that, optional monthly maintenance packages start from Ksh 5,000/month for updates, security, and performance monitoring.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes! Registered non-profits and educational institutions receive a 15% discount on all services. Just mention it during our consultation and provide documentation.

Can I pay via MPesa?

Absolutely — MPesa is my primary payment method for local clients. Bank transfer and PayPal are also available for larger projects or international clients.

How accurate is the cost estimator?

It gives a solid ballpark — typically within 20% of the final quote. The exact price depends on specific requirements discussed during the free consultation. The estimator helps you budget and plan before we talk.
